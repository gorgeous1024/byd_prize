# Environment Images

Place environmental and technology-related images in this directory.

## Required Images

### Green Technology Image
- **Filename**: `green-technology.jpg`
- **Usage**: AboutCause section
- **Recommended size**: 1000x1000px (1:1 aspect ratio)
- **Subject**: Environmental imagery, green technology, sustainable innovation

## Image Requirements
- **Format**: JPG or WebP
- **Quality**: High resolution, professional photography
- **File size**: 300-700KB
- **Theme**: Sustainability, nature, clean energy, technology

## Suggested Subjects
- Wind turbines or solar panels
- Green forests or natural landscapes
- Electric vehicle charging stations
- Clean technology manufacturing
- Urban green spaces
- Hands holding plants or seedlings
- Modern sustainable architecture
