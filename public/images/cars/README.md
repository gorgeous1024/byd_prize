# Car Images

Place car product images in this directory.

## Required Images

### Main Hero Image
- **Filename**: `byd-seal-u.jpg`
- **Usage**: Hero section main image
- **Recommended size**: 1200x800px (3:2 aspect ratio)
- **Subject**: 2025 BYD SEAL U - front 3/4 view preferred

## Image Requirements
- **Format**: JPG or WebP
- **Quality**: High resolution, professional product photography
- **File size**: 200-500KB
- **Background**: Clean, professional setting

## Tips
- Use professional automotive photography
- Ensure good lighting and clear view of the vehicle
- Consider multiple angles if you plan to add more car images
- Maintain consistent style across all car images
